HEY THERE, CHOOM!

Welcome to Cyberpunk 2077 Chats.

Here you will find fan-made chats between characters from Cyberpunk 2077 using a custom UI based on that seen in the Cyberpunk 2077 video game. This project is of course, a work in progress and there is still a lot to do. However, I do hope you enjoy reading the chats I have created.

Cyberpunk 2077 Chats will be updated periodically. You can also keep updated by following me on Twitter @rexxgta


CHATS

Chats are classed into V (Female) and V (Male). To begin, simply choose which gender of V you'd like to read as. Chats with specific characters will change depending on V's gender. If a character can be romanced by the chosen gender, separate chats will be made with different dialogues.



Coding by REXX, based on the original Cyberpunk 2077 design by CD Projekt Red.
